About

“The blueprint for The Heavy Sounds is simple: Keep the people dancing. Steeped in the tradition of 60's and early 70's instrumental Soul and Funk, The Heavy Sounds have quite literally created a band that can say everything while saying nothing.”

John Wills - guitar
Dan Ingenthron - organ
Jeremy Kay - bass
Ryan Juravic - drums

Contact

The Heavy Sounds are located in Chicago, IL.
When they're not playing instrumentals they back up Chicago soul legend Renaldo Domino.

For booking and other inquiries, contact theheavysoundsband@gmail.com
